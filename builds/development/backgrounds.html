Flexy

A FlexBox based framework.

Flexy Backgrounds

Flexy comes with a number of background options. First there are varying heights: bg-full, bg-half and bg-fourth. Text within a section containing a bg-* class will automatically center vertically and inverse in color.

Patterns & Colors

Flexy also comes with a few color and shape options. By default, bg-full, half and fourth will be your your default "Callout" color.
